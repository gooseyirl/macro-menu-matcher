Items made without gluten-containing ingredients, by restaurant.

⚠ Important: Not certified gluten-free All items listed here are made without gluten-containing ingredients according to each chain's official allergen information. However, none of these restaurants operate dedicated gluten-free kitchens — cross-contamination from shared fryers, preparation surfaces, and utensils is possible. Subway's GF bread is prepared in the same kitchen as wheat bread. If you have coeliac disease or a severe gluten intolerance, contact the restaurant directly before ordering.
